Here's a guide on using a mouse:



Mouse Usage:


1. Left-click: Press the left mouse button to perform actions like selecting, opening, or activating items.
2. Right-click: Press the right mouse button to access context menus and perform specific actions related to the selected item.
3. Scroll wheel: Roll the scroll wheel to navigate through documents, web pages, or lists. Some mice also have additional buttons that can be customized for specific functions.
4. Drag and drop: Click and hold the left mouse button on an item, then move the mouse to a new location. Release the button to drop the item into the new location.
5. Double-click: Quickly press the left mouse button twice in succession to open files, folders, or launch applications.
6. Hover: Move the mouse cursor over an item without clicking to display additional information or trigger certain actions (e.g., tooltips).
